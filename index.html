How To Play
To Launch The Game
  • Click on the green play button
  • Choose your difficulty
  • Enjoy!!!
How To Play
  • The objective is to find all the matching pairs before the time runs out
  • Pick 2 cards by clicking on them, the cards will be turned over
  • If they match, the cards will be highlighted and then select 2 different cards
  • If not then they will be turned back
